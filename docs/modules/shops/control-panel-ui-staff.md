# Shop Staff (Inside Control Panel)

# Shop Staff Management

Shop Staff allows shop owners to add or remove players who can manage their shop.

This feature is accessed through the Control Panel under the Shop Keeper section.

---

## Where It Is Located

Inside:

Control Panel UI → Shop Keeper → Staff

This opens a player selection GUI.

---

## What It Does

Shop Staff allows:

* Adding players as shop staff
* Removing players from shop staff
* Granting management permissions without transferring ownership

Staff members may be allowed to:

* Change price
* Change stock
* Toggle modes
* Manage inventory
* View history

Permissions depend on configuration.

---

## Player Selection Menu

When managing staff:

* A player selection GUI opens
* You can search or select players
* Clicking toggles their staff status

This makes it easy to manage large team shops.

## Recommended Use

Shop Staff is ideal for:

* Town shops
* Nation shops
* Team-based economy servers
* Marketplace districts
* Staff-supervised markets

It allows delegation without losing control.
